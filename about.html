About Me

I am Shohruz, a dedicated and ambitious student at the University of Oklahoma, pursuing a BBA in Management Information Systems (MIS) alongside an MS in Management Information Technology. Expected to graduate in May 2025, I’m passionate about leveraging technology to solve business challenges.

I was born in Tajikistan and completed my high school education at UWC Dilijan in Armenia, where I studied with students from over 85 countries and earned my International Baccalaureate (IB) diploma. This diverse environment helped shape my global perspective and fostered my commitment to collaborative problem-solving.

My professional experience spans internships at Midland Mortgage and American Fidelity Assurance, where I contributed to projects involving data analysis, automation, and improving technical processes. At Midland Mortgage, I developed an extensive framework using Python, MS Access, and VBA to evaluate loan pools, significantly supporting the company's live bidding processes.

At the University of Oklahoma, I’ve been recognized on the President's and Dean’s Honor Roll and received the prestigious PACE Award, which acknowledges 1% of the freshman class for outstanding participation, academic achievement, community involvement, and leadership excellence.

Fluent in Tajik, Persian, English, and Russian, I bring a global perspective to every project and am committed to driving innovation and making a positive impact through technology.
